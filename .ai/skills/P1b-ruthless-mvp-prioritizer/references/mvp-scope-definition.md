# MVP Scope Definition (Milestone 1)

## IN SCOPE (MUST HAVE)

- CSV/Excel upload
- Pay gap calculation (median by gender)
- EVG scoring (GPT-4o)
- Article 16 reporting (quartile analysis)
- RODO compliance (N<3 masking)
- Partner portal (basic: client list, onboard)
- Manual EVG override (EU AI Act)

## OUT OF SCOPE (Defer)

- Dark mode
- Mobile app
- AI chatbot
- Slack integration
- Market benchmarking
- White-label branding
- SSO/SAML
- ERP integrations
- Blockchain anything
