# EU Directive 2023/970 - Critical Excerpts

## Article 4: Equal pay for the same work or work of equal value

Employers shall ensure equal pay for female and male workers for the same work or work of equal value.

## Article 7: Right to information

Workers shall have the right to receive, in writing, information on:
(a) their individual pay level
(b) the average pay levels, broken down by sex

## Article 9: Pay reporting

Employers employing at least 100 workers shall provide information on the pay gap between female and male workers.

## Article 16: Right to compensation

Member States shall ensure that workers who have suffered harm as a result of an infringement have access to full compensation or reparation.
